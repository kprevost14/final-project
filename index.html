Our Story

Riverside Art Center was founded to inspire creativity through the arts. Located in beautiful & historic downtown Wapakoneta, OH. Riverside Art Center is home to a variety of art classes which adults can sign up for to improve their artistic side and explore their creativity.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to serves the greater Wapakoneta area, providing art related, educational opportunities through classes, lectures and demonstrations. Riverside Art Center also supports local small business by giving 30% of each payment made towards their classes given.
